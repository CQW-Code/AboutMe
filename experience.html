Past Experience & Goals

My first job was as a Communication Specialist working with deaf and nonverbal residents of a residential facility for intellectually disabled children and adults. It was here I got into computers more (along with my first computer as a college graduation gift). I later became a Case Manager taking on most of the deaf and non-verbal clients in the building I worked in, then changed to a different set- those with dual diagnoses- mental illness AND intellectual disability- teenagers too!


I then left that job and attended The Chubb Institute in Pennsylvania- back then that was considered a "boot camp"- 7 month program in learning COBOL, C++ and Visual Basic 4 (I'm old!).


After that I became a Quality Assurance tester working on Mainframe COBOL applications and CICS screens. I moved around to many states- Colorado, Georgia, Arizona and South Carolina and in many industries- health insurance, MCI World Com and even a short stint with FireVine testing web pages.


My goals for this course is to add to my skills and to move a bit further into the present and future... and not fall flat on my face. :)
