What I do for fun

I enjoy playing online point and click games as well as online riddle trails. I've played many over the years including a large team based one called Invasion. Invasion was started in 2006 by a wife and husband team (no longer together).


Invasion involved several teams of players in a race to solve "rounds" given to us by the creators. We were to solve anything from Polybus Squares to deciphering photos and hidden clues. Sometimes we'd get 'egg pages' to steer us in the right direction. The goal was reached when we solved the final page and a destination was given. The beauty of this game was that we were to go out and get a picture of the destination with our team sign. This is where having many people from all over the world on our team becomes an advantage! We had folks from the US, UK, Belgium, Romania, Spain, Canada and India to name a few!
